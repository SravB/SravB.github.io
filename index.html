About Me

I'm currently a Computer Science & Business Double Degree student at the University of Waterloo. I completed my first internship at BMO Capital Markets during the Summer of 2018. I gained experience in full stack development, machine learning and blockchains. It was especially exciting because I was one of the primary software engineers that created the blockchain platform used in the first blockchain bond issuance in Canada. This initiative resulted in a boost in BMO’s credit rating. There are numerous press releases about the project - some notable articles include Bloomberg, CoinDesk and Financial Post.

I'm a fast learner which allows me to be flexible with my technologies as necessary. I'm looking for internships and other learning opportunities so if you need another team member, feel free to contact me!
Aside from mathematics and computer science, I invest my time in basketball, track & field, weightlifting, chess, drawing and reading.
